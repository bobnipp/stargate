Acceptable Formats:

Decimal Degrees
  • up to 7 digits after the decimal
  • example with plus/minus: DD.DDDDDDD -DDD.DDDDDDD
  • example with direction: DD.DDDDDDDX DDD.DDDDDDDX
Degrees minutes seconds
  • D=Degrees, M=Minutes, S=Seconds, X=Direction
  • example: DDMMSSX DDDMMSSX
Decimal Degrees (separate multiple vertices with a comma or semi-colon)
  • up to 7 digits after the decimal
  • example with plus/minus: DD.DDDDD -DDD.DDDDD, DD.DDDDD -DDD.DDDD
  • example with direction: DD.DDDDDX DDD.DDDDX, DD.DDDDX DDD.DDDDX
Degrees minutes seconds (separate multiple vertices with a comma or semi-colon)
  • D=Degrees, M=Minutes, S=Seconds, X=Direction
  • example: DDMMSSX DDDMMSSX, DDMMSSX DDDMMSSX
